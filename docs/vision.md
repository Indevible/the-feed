# Vision

The Feed turns excess precision in XDEL activity into communication.

A transfer, swap, or trade can carry two things at once:

1. economic value;
2. an appended numeric expression.

For example:

`105.00143 XDEL`

may carry a real amount while also ending in a recognizable code.

The number should communicate independently of price. The same expression may appear at different scales, allowing a nearly free gesture or a coded appendix to a meaningful transaction.

The Feed is intended to become entertaining public infrastructure:

- a moving web Feed;
- live Discord output;
- live Telegram output;
- shareable meme cards;
- public links back to source activity;
- a language people learn by seeing and using.

The Feed is not a price dashboard, trading signal service, reputation score, or custody system.

Its purpose is interaction.

Mystery may surround the language at first. Clarity should emerge through real examples, light public posts, and visible behavior rather than a single exhaustive explanation.

## Core language

> People may want XDEL for the object. We keep it for the objective.

> The market may notice XDEL. Indelible notices how the market uses it.

> In the journey from Feed to Steed, real respect should be guaranteed.

The market-and-use line remains open to refinement, but its direction is fixed: Indelible is interested in interaction, not price action.
