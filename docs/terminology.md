# Draft terminology

All terms are provisional until the underlying mechanics are defined.

## The Feed

The moving public presentation layer where interpreted XDEL activity appears, scrolls, and may reappear when later interaction changes its meaning.

## Veme

A coded expression carried by wallet-to-wallet XDEL activity.

## Exeme

A possible term for coded XDEL activity occurring through an exchange, pool, or swap.

## Trademe

A possible term for coded buy or sell activity where trade direction contributes to the meaning.

## Vouch

A deliberate, durable recognition of standing or integrity.

Vouches are not casual Feed events. Their mechanics are developed separately and are outside this repository.

## Open gesture

A recognized one-way expression that may appear briefly in The Feed without creating any obligation to respond.

## Reciprocal interaction

A later response from the original recipient that links to an earlier gesture and may return the completed interaction to the top of The Feed.
