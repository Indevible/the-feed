# Core language

These lines are being preserved as the first compact expression of the Indelible vision.

## Object and objective

> People may want XDEL for the object. We keep it for the objective.

## Market and use

> The market may notice XDEL. Indelible notices how the market uses it.

This line is preserved as a working formulation. Its meaning is established even if the final wording changes: Indelible is concerned with interaction and use, not price movement.

## Feed to Steed

> In the journey from Feed to Steed, real respect should be guaranteed.

Together, these lines describe three layers:

- the discipline of the project;
- the observation of interaction;
- the longer journey from activity toward durable standing.
