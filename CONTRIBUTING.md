# Contributing

The Feed is in early public design.

Discussion, criticism, examples, collision reports, and terminology proposals are welcome.

## Useful contributions now

- identify ambiguous numeric patterns;
- propose clearer parsing rules;
- identify accidental-match risks;
- suggest community-safe presentation patterns;
- document Discord or Telegram integration needs;
- propose balanced expressive categories.

## Boundaries

Do not submit:

- wallet seeds, private keys, or signing code;
- custody or treasury automation;
- price-promotion mechanics;
- negative reputation scoring;
- accusations or blacklist systems;
- Vouch permanence mechanics;
- copied meme assets without clear rights.
